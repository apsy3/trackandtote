Section 4

How to read this project

  1. Read frequency first. Annual and versioned datasets cannot answer daily questions.
  2. Interpret across clocks. Air quality, rainfall, emissions, and OHSE series move at different speeds.
  3. Use confidence and status labels to calibrate claims and avoid overstatement.
  4. Treat this as an evolving public notebook while source licensing and governance move toward connector-by-connector validation.
