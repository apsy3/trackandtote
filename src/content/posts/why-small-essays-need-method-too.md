Short essays are often treated as purely opinion. But a short essay can still have structure.

In this project, each note tries to make one move:

1. observe a pattern
2. locate a source
3. state a limit

That small discipline creates a bridge between general readers and policy-minded readers. Both can stay in the same conversation without the writing becoming inaccessible.
