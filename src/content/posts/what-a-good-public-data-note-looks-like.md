Good public-data writing is not about adding many charts. It is about adding the *right* chart at the right moment.

## Minimal checklist

1. Clear question
2. Clear source
3. Clear unit and timeframe
4. Clear caveat

If one item is missing, the piece can still be published, but the gap must be visible to the reader.

That transparency is part of trust.
