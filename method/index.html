Method

Readable writing, disciplined evidence

Track & Tote keeps the tone public-facing while preserving method guardrails around source governance, time context, and claim language.

Source-first

Every series carries source and source URL context so readers can trace where claims begin.

Time-aware

Frequency, latest observation, and retrieval timestamp are visible before interpretation.

Mock until verified

We do not label data as live until licence, attribution, cache, and refresh rules are validated.

No false precision

Charts are used as visual notes with confidence and status labels, not as proof by styling.
