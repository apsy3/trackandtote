Method

How we keep it clear and lawful

Each article is published only after source, license, and claim-scope checks.

1. Question first

Start with a focused public question before selecting a chart.

2. Source checks

Confirm license, attribution terms, update cadence, and known data gaps.

3. Match scale

Align geography and timeframe before comparing variables.

4. Publish caveats

State what the evidence can show and what it cannot prove.
