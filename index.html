What this becomes

A growing public-data journal.

Track & Tote starts with one disciplined question: how rainfall, groundwater, crop production, and market prices move together without pretending every pattern is proof.

  1. 01

    Define the hypothesis

    State what we expect to observe before looking for a chart that agrees.

  2. 02

    Audit the data

    Track source, license, geography, frequency, missing values, and known limits.

  3. 03

    Publish cautiously

    Show maps, charts, lag checks, and caveats before making strong claims.
