What this becomes

A growing public-data journal.

Track & Tote starts with the reusable-source inventory and then turns selected rows into disciplined studies. That keeps each article grounded in licence, quality, cadence, and coverage before any chart becomes a claim.

  1. 01

    Define the hypothesis

    Pick a question from sources that are website-ready and time-aligned.

  2. 02

    Audit the data

    Track source, license, risk level, geography, frequency, and known limits.

  3. 03

    Publish cautiously

    Show maps, charts, lag checks, and caveats before making strong claims.
