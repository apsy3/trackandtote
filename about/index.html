About

Why this exists

Track & Tote is a personal writing space for short essays, field notes, and public-policy reflections. Some pieces are text-first. Some include charts. The intention is always the same: readable writing with honest method.

What readers should expect

Clear language, visible assumptions, and sources that can be checked.

No inflated certainty. No decorative data for its own sake.
